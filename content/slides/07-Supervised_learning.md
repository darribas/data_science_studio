## Modeling

<CENTER>
$$
y = f(X)
$$
</CENTER>

**`Regression`** $\; \rightarrow \; f(X) = \alpha + \beta X + \epsilon$

**`CART`** $\; \rightarrow \; f(X) =$ Tree

**`Random Forest`** $\; \rightarrow \; f(X) =$ Tree Ensemble

**`Gradient boost`** $\; \rightarrow \; f(X) =$ Sequence of predictors (e.g. trees)
